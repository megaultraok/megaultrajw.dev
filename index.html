About Me

Hi! I'm Jada, a software engineer currently working as a cloud ERP developer.

Although I presently work in the IT industry, I've always had an interest in programming and product-based software development since my adolescence. Technology and its inner-workings intrigued me so much, I decided to obtain a B.A. in Computer Science!

While in college, I've interned as a data analysis research assistant in its neuroscience department, and as a product development intern for a grant-funded, student-run data science consultancy. With this, I've had a taste of the data science discipline, and its insight to and impact on healthcare and business decisions. Today, my main interest is in cloud computing — which has been heavily influenced by my occupation — but also by the rapid growth and inclusion of AI and online storage management in our daily lives.

I like challenges of solving real-world problems by software engineering usable tools for not just the technically advanced, but for the everyday person. Creating products that are practical yet functional is my go-to template. However, I'm always eager to learn new technologies and collaborate to expand my knowledge and develop a high-value product.

Besides any technical pastimes, I love reading nonfictional articles and books, watching documentaries and video biographies, astronomy, and singing!
